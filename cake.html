Ice Cream Cake

Description

This cake is the best dessert loved by both adults and children. It is delicious, chocolatey, sweet and very fancy!

What can we add? This recipe's name speaks for itself. This cake is the best present for a party or a family dinner. It does not require any cooking and can be made quickly.

Ingredients

  • 137g pkt chocolate sandwich biscuits with vanilla creme
  • 50g butter, melted
  • 1L Coles Salted Caramel ice cream, softened
  • 4 chocolate-coated chocolate-cream centre biscuits, coarsely chopped
  • 1 chocolate-coated bar, caramel and peanut-flavoured, coarsely chopped
  • 1L Coles Belgian Chocolate Ripple ice cream, softened
  • 2 x 40g nougat bars, coated in chocolate & coconut, coarsely chopped
  • 4 chocolate-coated wafer balls with hazelnut centres, coarsely chopped
  • Chocolate fudge topping, to serve
  • Assorted chocolates, to serve
  • Assorted biscuits, to serve

Steps

  1. Grease a 20cm (base measurement) springform pan and line base and side with baking paper. Place chocolate sandwich biscuits with vanilla creme in a food processor and process until finely crushed. Add butter and process until combined. Spoon into prepared pan and use the back of a spoon to press the biscuit mixture over the base. Place in fridge for 15 mins or until firm.
  2. Combine salted caramel ice cream, chocolate-coated chocolate-cream centre biscuits and chocolate-coated caramel and peanut bar in a bowl. Spoon over the biscuit base in the pan. Smooth the surface. Freeze for 30 mins.
  3. Combine the chocolate ice cream, chopped nougat bars and chopped wafer balls in a large bowl. Spoon over the caramel ice cream and smooth the surface. Place in the freezer for 6 hours or until set.
  4. Transfer the cake to a serving plate. Drizzle with the chocolate topping. Top with the assorted chocolates and biscuits and serve immediately.
  5. Enjoy!
