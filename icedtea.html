Southern-style Sweet Iced-Tea with lemon and mint

Description

What can be better than a tall glass of cold and refreshing tea during a hot summer day? This recipe of a home-made iced tea is incredibly easy and cheap, yet much fresher and more delicious than its alternatives from the store.

The recipe is flexible and can be modified. You can add whatever you want, for example fruits, berries, more sugar, syrups, or even herbs!

Ingredients

  • 8 cups filtered water
  • 6 bags black tea of choice
  • 1/3 cups of sugar, adjust to taste (optional)
  • 1 lemon sliced
  • 1 cup fresh mint

Steps

  1. Bring half of the water to a boil in a saucepan. Remove from heat and add tea bags. Allow the tea bags to steep for 8-11 minutes (according to desired intensity).
  2. Remove the tea bags from the water. Note: If you prefer sweet tea, add the sugar while the water is still hot.
  3. Add the remaining water to the tea. Leave it refrigerated until chilled (about an hour).
  4. In a tall glass, add ice, lemon slices and fresh mint. Pour the chilled tea inside.
  5. Enjoy!
