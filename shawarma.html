Shawarma with chicken

Description

This delicious dish came from the Middle East and is extremely popular in the countries of Europe and Asia. It has everything you need: fresh vegetables, a thin-sliced juicy chicken, a tasty flatbread, and a lot of aromatic hand-made sauce.

It can be made in a couple of minutes and is very addictive. Probably that's why this is one of the few meals that can be found in both fancy restaurants and fast food joints.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken thigh fillets
  • 1 cup Greek yoghurt
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed
  • 6 flatbreads (pita breads can be used)
  • 2 tomatoes sliced
  • 2 cucumbers sliced
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp ground coriander, cardamon, cayenne pepper
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • Salt, pepper

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine coriander, cardamon, cayenne pepper, paprika, lemon juice, olive oil. Add 1 tsp of ground cumin and the minced garlic.
  2. Mix the ingredients
  3. Add the chicken and make sure each piece is coated with the marinade.
  4. Marinate overnight or up to 24 hours.
  5. Get another bowl. Add inside the Greek yoghurt, the crushed garlic, 1 tsp of ground cumin and a squeeze of lemon juice. Add salt and pepper.
  6. Mix the sauce and put it inside the fridge until needed.
  7. Heat the grill or pan on medium high.
  8. Place chicken on the grill and cook the first side for 4 to 5 minutes until nicely charred, then turn and cook the other side for 3 to 4 minutes.
  9. Remove chicken from the grill and cover loosely with foil. Set aside to rest for 5 minutes.
  10. Slice chicken and pile onto platter alongsite the flatbreads, sauce, and sliced vegetables.
  11. Add some sauce and sliced chicken onto the flatbread. Place the vegetables on top of it and wrap the shawarma.
  12. Enjoy!
